Lake Kegonsa Interactive

David Waro, Angela Baldocchi, Ben Polchowski

This tool was created to bridge the gap between Lake Kegonsa's rich past and current efforts to maintain the lake's health. These sites hold historical significance, are locations of current or future projects, are simply popular sites bolstered by Kegonsa's surrounding community, or are locations where events took place that greatly impacted the lake's water quality. Simply click on a marker to explore its role in forming Lake Kegonsa's identity.
